On Fire to Hire: Start-Up Expo

Register for Event Save Event

Event Description

Are you fired-up to work for a startup?

Network with startups seeking to hire for:

  • The Fall 2016 co-op cycle
  • Full-time Positions
  • Internships

Northeastern University students and alumni are invited. NU ID required.

Tuesday, October 25 - 5:30PM-7:30PM - Curry Student Center Ballroom & Quad

To obtain a listing of participating ventures, visit: www.northeastern.edu/startupexpo & click on "Participating Startups"

The event is coordinated and sponsored by the Northeastern University Center for Entrepreneurship Education in partnership with the College of Computer & Information Science, Cooperative Education and Career Development, the D'Amore-McKim School of Business & the Michael J. and Ann Sherman Center for Engineering Entrepreneurship Education.
