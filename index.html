Northeastern Co-Op: An Overview

At Northeastern, the Cooperative-Education (Co-Op) program is a key aspect of most students lives at some point throughout their time at the university. Co-Op is a distinctive educational approach which integrates rigorous classroom study with real world experiences to create a powerful way to learn. The program offers students the ability to work in real world job settings instead of attending traditional classroom courses.

Due to the fact that this site was designed to help students manage their individualized Co-Op search, it is required for users of this site to have a Husky Email - meaning they must be enrolled in the university. For more information on how to apply to Northeastern, visit their Admissions page located on the Northeastern website.

Benefits to Using NU:OnTrack

Get all of your tasks done on time! Using the Co-Op Tracker allows you to manage all of your Co-Op applications easily and effictivley. Never again be caught off guard when application deadlines role around!

Plan your events and interviews with ease! This site offers users a simple way to quickly view all important information regarding any aspect of the Co-Op process.

Track your progress and status on various job postings, applications, and interview sessions. We give users the tools necessary to see how well they are completing their tasks and where they might need to devote more time to nail that final interview!
